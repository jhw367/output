Commercial activation gate

A route becomes commercially active only when at least 3 of 4 are true:

  • named owner or plausible owner-route;
  • cross-system workflow or exception pain;
  • timing, funding, programme or regulatory pressure;
  • existing platform does not fully solve the issue.

Next 10 working days

  • Create/maintain the Truviq gBrain: scoring, disqualifiers, vocabulary and call-note templates.
  • For each pod, choose maximum two routes and close exactly one evidence gap.
  • Convert qualifying routes into a 20-minute workflow-fit meeting request.
  • Classify every outcome as pursue, nurture, evidence gap or disqualify.
  • Keep D-leads monitor-only.

Demolish check

  • Many high scores are still segment or ecosystem signals, not proven buying intent.
  • Existing EPD/ECD/IAM/triage tooling may already solve the workflow state.
  • VZVZ/Nictiz-style routes provide authority and access, but not automatically budget.
  • Policy pressure such as AZWA/KIK-V can be real without immediate IT procurement.
