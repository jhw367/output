Evolving Worldview

The world is converging toward AI-mediated institutions controlled by a small number of compute-intensive platforms. The decisive question is no longer whether organizations will use AI, but whether they can retain sovereignty, accountability and public trust while relying on external intelligence infrastructure.

Countries and institutions that master procurement, governance and operational deployment will gain disproportionate strategic advantage. Those that only regulate without implementing will become dependent on others.
