Why the 1958 marriage record is not appearing publicly

HGA and Open Archieven both state that Dutch civil marriage acts become public only after 75 years. This does not prove the Hamer × Kuling marriage or the identities behind the initials, but it explains why repeated public-index searches in 2026 may return no result for a 27 December 1958 act.
