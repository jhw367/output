What is currently source-backed?

1926 marriage: Hamer × Van den Oever

Hubertus Josephus Emmanuel Marie Hamer married Theodora Regina Maria van den Oever in The Hague. The official civil-register source confirms full names, Hubertus’s profession as mining engineer, and both sets of parents.

Source: S006 · A — direct civil-register evidence

1942 Poelau Bras death context

Hubertus Josephus Emmanuel Marie Hamer is externally confirmed as deceased on 7 March 1942 in the Indian Ocean aboard s.s. Poelau Bras. This supports the family story and links the line to the wartime evacuation disaster.

Source: S008 · A — external OGS/National Archives dossier index

1987 family-notice context

Family notices for Theodora Regina Maria van den Oever describe her as widow of Hubertus J.E.M. Hamer and provide family-context clues for the next generation.

Source: S003/S009 · B — family notices / OCR; useful but not civil-register proof

Kuling line lead
